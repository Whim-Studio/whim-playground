# Credits & attribution

## Art & audio

All graphics in this project are **placeholder art drawn programmatically with
`Graphics2D`** (colored polygons, simple icons). No externally-sourced art or
audio is used at this time.

If freely-licensed assets (CC0 / public-domain, e.g. from OpenGameArt.org) are
added later, each will be credited here with its source and license. Original
pixel art authored for this project will likewise be noted here.
