# Credits & attribution

## Art & audio

All graphics in this project are **placeholder art drawn programmatically with
`Graphics2D`** (colored polygons, tracker cylinders, simple icons, typography).
No externally-sourced art or audio is used. There are no asset files and the app
never loads images.

## Original game

*Tipping Point* is © its designer **Ryan Smith** and publisher **Treecer** (2020).
This is an independent, non-commercial, clean-room fan recreation of the game's
**rules and mechanics** (which are not copyrightable). It contains **none** of the
original game's artwork, component graphics, or text, and does not depend on any of
its files. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Ryan Smith or Treecer.
